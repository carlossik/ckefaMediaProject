About CKEFA Media

CKEFA Media is dedicated to bringing grassroots and amateur football to the forefront with high-quality recording and live streaming services. Our mission is to ensure that every match, no matter the level, gets the professional attention it deserves, providing players, coaches, and fans with exceptional media coverage.

Our Services

1. Match Recording

CKEFA Media specializes in capturing every moment of grassroots and amateur football matches. Using state-of-the-art equipment, our team ensures that every game is recorded in high definition, allowing you to relive the action with crystal-clear quality. Whether it's a local league match or a friendly, we provide comprehensive coverage that showcases the skill and excitement of the game.

2. Live Streaming

Experience the thrill of live football with our streaming services. We broadcast matches live on popular social media platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, and X (formerly Twitter), making it easy for fans to watch their favorite teams in real-time. Our live streaming service is designed to be seamless and accessible, ensuring that supporters can stay connected with the action no matter where they are.

3. Game Analysis

Take your team's performance to the next level with our detailed game analysis services. Our experts review match footage to provide in-depth insights and feedback. Coaches and players can benefit from our comprehensive analysis, which highlights key moments, strategies, and areas for improvement. With CKJEFA Media, you'll gain valuable perspectives that can help enhance your game.

4. Highlights

Capture the essence of each match with our professionally edited highlights. Our team selects the best moments from every game, creating engaging and dynamic highlight reels that celebrate the excitement and intensity of football. These highlights are perfect for sharing on social media, team websites, and with friends and family.

5. In-Game Photography

Preserve the memories of each match with our professional in-game photography services. Our photographers capture high-quality images of key moments, players, and action shots, providing you with stunning photos that highlight the intensity and emotion of the game.
